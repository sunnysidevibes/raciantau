<h2 style='font-family:"SingleSleeve";font-size:48pt;text-align:center;'>Boiling</h2>
<h3>by Iris Flores</h3>
<br />
<p>One day</p>
<p>Looking up towards the sky</p>
<p>It hits me</p>
<p>We look <em>out</em> towards the sky</p>
<p>Not unlike</p>
<p>out towards the sea</p>
<br />
<p>Across an ancient, alien medium</p>
<p>I witness stars — islands far away from home</p>
<p>The clouds roll in, high tide</p>
<p>I’m safe ashore, but! Passion</p>
<p>Don’t we all want to explore?</p>
<br />
<p>Explore my Self and what I found is nothing</p>
<p>An ancient, alien, empty thing</p>
<p>Maybe, reaching out towards the stars</p>
<p>I find meaning</p>
<p>I find life</p>
<br />
<p>But Death — our mother</p>
<p>Her grasp is more than strong she’s perfect</p>
<br />
<p>I find pain</p>
<p>I find struggle</p>
<p>I find all we know is only but a dream</p>
<br />
<p>But Death — She calls us down (no. <em>in</em>)</p>
<p>Her hand comes up (no. <em>out</em>) and pulls us</p>
<br />
<p>A tree</p>
<p>Grows tall</p>
<p>It lives</p>
<p>It dies</p>
<p>Comes back home</p>
<p>It melts into the ground</p>
<br />
<p>A body</p>
<p>Grows old</p>
<p>It suffers</p>
<p>It laughs</p>
<p>Shits itself before it dies</p>
<p>It melts into the ground</p>
<br />
<p>A person</p>
<p>Grows sad</p>
<p>They suffer</p>
<p>On a building or, perhaps, a bridge</p>
<p>(or maybe, sitting on a tub, holding in their hand a knife?)</p>
<p>They jump — out towards the stars</p>
<br />
<p>But Death — She calls us down (no. <em>in</em>)</p>
<p>Her hand comes up (no. <em>out</em>) and pulls us</p>
<br />
<p>Life, in the scope of all things true — so short</p>
<p>Not tall</p>
<p>We know how far away the stars are</p>
<p>Like us, lonely</p>
<p>Death, in the scope of all things true — alive</p>
<br />
<p>Soil</p>
<p>The womb of all things on this lonely rock</p>
<p>This boiling rock atop a furnace (a God, the Sun)</p>
<p>We call it Earth and we are bubbles</p>
<p>Sea foam blooming on this beach’s sand</p>
